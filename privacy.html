1. Server-Side Data Protection

Enhanced Privacy: Server-Side Tagging Active

Unlike standard websites, we use a Server-Side Tagging Gateway (sGTM). This acts as a protective buffer between your browser and third-party tools (like Google Analytics). This allows us to:

  • Anonymize your IP address before it is stored or shared.
  • Strip sensitive personal identifiers from tracking requests.
  • Ensure no tracking occurs until you have provided explicit consent.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

We only process non-essential data (Analytics and Marketing) if you give us your Explicit Consent via our cookie banner. Essential cookies are processed based on Legitimate Interests to keep the site secure and functional.

3. Your Rights
